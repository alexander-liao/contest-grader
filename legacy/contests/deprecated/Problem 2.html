Problem 2: Don't Forget to Cache!

On the most recent CCC, Alex forgot to cache his results for S4, thus losing him a whole two points. He found a different problem that needs caching and he wants to try to solve this one to redeem himself.

Input Specifications

The first line will contain a single integer N.

Output Specifications

The output will consist of a single integer, the Nth fibonacci number modulo 10 ** 9 + 7. f(1) = 1 and f(2) = 1 and f(3) = 2.
The definition of the Fibonacci sequence is by the function f where f(n) = f(n - 1) + f(n - 2) and f(1) = f(2) = 1.

Subtasks

For 20% of the points, N ≤ 100
For another 20% of the points, N ≤ 1000
For the remaining 60% of the points, N ≤ 100 000

Sample Input

12

Sample Output

144

Sample Input

100

Sample Output

687995182
